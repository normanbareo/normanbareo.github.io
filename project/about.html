About Us

Niantic, Inc.

Niantic, Inc. is an American software development company based in San Francisco. It is best known for developing the augmented reality mobile games Ingress, Pokémon GO, and Harry Potter: Wizards Unite.

Niantic's mission is to build augmented reality experiences that encourage outdoor exploration and social interaction.

Nintendo Co., Ltd.

Nintendo Co., Ltd. is a Japanese multinational consumer electronics and video game company headquartered in Kyoto. It is one of the world's largest video game companies by market capitalization, creating some of the best-known and top-selling video game franchises, such as Mario, The Legend of Zelda, and Pokémon.

Nintendo is also the creator of Pokémon, which is jointly owned by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures.

Certified Shifters - TC02

Certified Shifters is a group of five IT students from FEU - Institute of Technology: Norman Bareo, Luis Habon, Mico Larracas, Jericho Santos, and Miguel Taduran."

The group is currently enrolled in a course named IT0043: Web Design with Client-Side Scripting. They have been tasked with redesigning the Pokémon GO Web Store as part of the final project for the course.
